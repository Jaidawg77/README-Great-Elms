Great Elms

This is a text based game inspired by David Lynch and Mark Frost's cult classic show 'Twin Peaks'. 
The story follows Detective Philip J. Gordon and his mission to find missing 18 year old Daisy Oddly in the small seaside town of Great Elms.
Expect the unexpected and remember that the owls are not what they seem.

How to play: Use your keyboard and follow along :)

Installation: Thony or Python based enviroments
